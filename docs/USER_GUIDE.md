# User Guide (Employee)

## 1) Where do I see my score?
- Open **Dashboard → Employee Dashboard**
- You can see:
  - Your latest score
  - Trend over time
  - Optional anonymized comments (if enabled)

## 2) How do I complete an evaluation?
1) Open the notification for the evaluation
2) Answer the questions
3) (Optional) add a short comment
4) Submit

## 3) What does “Same” vs “Cross” mean?
- **Same department:** feedback from your own department
- **Cross department:** feedback from other departments

## 4) Anonymity
- If the evaluation is anonymous, your name is not shown to other users.
- Admin may have additional visibility depending on company settings.

## 5) Tips
- Answer honestly and professionally
- If you add a comment, keep it respectful and specific
